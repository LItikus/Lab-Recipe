Tasty Meal

Yummy Thin

Pancakes

Fast & Healthy To make a Breakfast Wealthy

Active Time: 10 min

Total Time: 30 min

The History of thin Pancakes

The pancakes or just pancakes - a dish of European cuisine, the kind of pancakes
cooked on milk without yeast sourdough. Typical for French cuisine and epecially
popular in Brittany and Central European cuisine. From Europe, they spread to the
Sputhern Cone, North Africa and the Latin American countries of the Southern Cone.
In the U.S., thanks to the neighborhood with Canada known under the French name
of cr'pe, but can be included in the Yiddish-borrowed concept of blintz along with
Russian panckes.

Ingredients

milk 500 mil

eggs 3 pcs.

flour 200g

butter (or vegetable)

30 g (2 tablespoons)

sugar 30 g (2 tablespoons)

salt 2-3 g (1/2 tsp)

Process

Step 1

Make a dough for homemade pancakes. Whisk eggs, sugar and salt in a bowl.

Step 2

Add half the milk and Stir.

Step 3

Addd flour little by little, stirring until the consistency of a homogeneous thick sour
cream.

Step 4

Add the rest of the milk and mix thoroughly. Add oil, stir and leave for 10-15
minutes

Step 5

Heat a cast-iron skillet, brush with vegetable oil and fry thin pancakes on both sides
over maximum heat.

Step 6

Spread them in a stack, brushing with small pieces of butter and sugar to taste.
Serve with jam and sour cream. Enjoy!
